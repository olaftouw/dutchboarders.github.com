Never give up!

If something does not work or is not it never give up! If we do fall sometimes very. Get up and try again and again. Pain is fine and blood is good.

Safety!

If you are a beginner and you've never have tried skateboarding or longboarding before. Try it with a knee and elbow pads because when the board is not yet fully under control do you fall regularly and that hurts. Continue practicing!
